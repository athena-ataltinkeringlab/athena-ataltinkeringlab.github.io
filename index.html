ATHENA GLOBAL SCHOOL is proud to introduce its Atal Tinkering Lab, a vibrant hub for innovation and creativity. At our lab, students are encouraged to explore, experiment, and invent, fostering a spirit of curiosity and problem-solving skills.

Explore the exciting world of STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) through hands-on projects, workshops, and collaborative learning experiences. Our Atal Lab provides state-of-the-art equipment and resources, empowering students to turn their ideas into reality.
